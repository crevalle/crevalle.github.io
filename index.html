Products

Crevalle is invested in making our client's products better. It is only natural for us to have the same passion for our own projects. Below are the projects Crevalle works on from time to time, trying to validate a business idea or simply create a simple, fun, and usable product.

  • Pulse - Simply track massive amounts of data simultaniously.

    An easy way to make your site metrics come alive. Built using Elixir this application can handle over 3 million simultanious connections without any scaling issues to worry about.

  • Chitta - A microjournal that tracks your thoughts and mood.

    The first step in improving one's mental health is to become aware of your mood throughout the day. Research shows documenting your mood using this simple grid leads to massive improvements.

  • Postfactor - Collaborate by refactoring pieces of code.

    There are many ways to solve the same problem. Post Factor open sources some of the decision making for you.

  • Decent Advice - Simple wholesome advice for professionals.

    A straightforward site dedicated to the communication of thoughtful advice. Applicable in your personal and professsional life.

  • Pixel Portrait - Who follows you says a lot.

    A project based upon creating a portrait that is made up entirely of your follower's avatars.
